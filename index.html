Royal Blue Tang

Royal Blue Tangs are rather flat, like a pancake, with a circular body shape, a pointed snout-like nose, and small scales.

  • Scientific Name: Paracanthurus hepatus
  • Average Length: 12 inches
  • Lifespan: 8 - 20 years
  • Habitat: Fragile Coral Reef

The Royal Blue Tangs typical weigh about 21.15 ounces. The reach about 12 inches in length and handling the tang risks the chances of being badly cut by the caudal spine
